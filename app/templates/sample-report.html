Strategic Snapshot

Understand how a competitor is positioned.

StoreScout summarizes market position, promo intensity, launch velocity, and catalog complexity so you can quickly understand how a Shopify store competes.

  • See whether a store leans budget, mid-market, or premium.
  • Spot how heavily they rely on promotions.
  • Use the snapshot as a quick starting point before digging deeper.
Pricing & Discounts

See pricing ranges and discount behavior.

Instead of manually checking products one by one, StoreScout breaks down price bands, median pricing, discounted product share, and discount depth.

  • Identify the competitor’s main price band.
  • Compare discount share against full-price products.
  • Understand whether sales are limited or aggressive.
Launch Timeline

Track how fast competitors are launching.

StoreScout shows new product activity across different time windows, helping you see whether a brand is accelerating, slowing down, or pushing a new strategy.

  • See new products from the last 30, 90, 180 days, and 12 months.
  • Compare new product pricing against the full catalog.
  • Spot premium expansion or catalog refresh patterns.
Notable Products

Find the products worth paying attention to.

Reports highlight the most expensive products and biggest discounts, making it easier to spot premium offers, bundles, markdowns, and potential positioning signals.

  • Quickly view top expensive products.
  • See which items have the biggest markdowns.
  • Use notable products to guide deeper competitor research.
